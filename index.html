The Walking Dead Best Side Characters

Ranked in no specific order

  • Daryl
  • Michonne
  • Glenn
  • Carl
  • Abraham
  • Carol
  • Maggie

Daryl. Daryl is one of the best characters in TWD. He had amazing character development and went from not even getting along with Rick to them becoming 'brothers' traveling alone for years after Rick disappeared to find him because he believed he is still alive. Daryl's fighting skills, tracking skills and loyalty to his friends made him one of the best and most useful charcaters in the walking dead.

Michonne. Michonne is an amazing character in TWD. She became a part of Ricks group in season 3 during their fight against 'The Govenor'. Her combat skills, confidence, bravery and relationship with Carl easily won over everyone's heart including Carl and Rick's.

Glenn. Glenn is a fan favorite in TWD. His kind and bright personality was loved by everyone in Ricks group. Glenn made his first appearance in season 1 when he helped save Rick from the walkers in Atlanta. His speed, scavenging skills, and quick thinking made him a needed character in TWD.

Carl. Carl is a strong character in the walking dead. He is the son of Rick and he faced many battles at such a young age in TWD which made many viewers sympatized with him. In season 1 you see Carl as a bratty little boy but in Season 8 you see him a strong reliable young man who's a leader just like his father. His relationship with Michonne was really cool to see. They both used each other as comfort after Carl lost his mom and Michonne lost her son. Carl's good judgement, combat skills, courageousness and kind heart made him easily one of the best TWD characters

Abraham. Abraham is a amazing TWD character. Abraham was the epitomy of bravery. When Abraham and the rest of Rick's team stood conered by the saviors and the BEST walking dead villain Negan he remained completely calm even right before his death. Abraham combat skills were amazing since he was a Sergeant in the U.S. Army before the apocolypse. His stubborness and hot headedness also made him a really funny character.

Carol. Carol is easily one of the strongest female TWD characters. She came a very long way since season 1. In season 1 Carol was being abused by her husband and did not have the confidence to stand up to him. Then her husband and daughter died and she wasn't sure what to do with her life. In later seasons we see Carol become better in combat and a reliable person in Ricks team. She became confident and was finally able to use her voice to stand up for herself and voice her opinions.

Maggie. Maggie was introduced in season 2 of TWD when Carl was shot and needed medical attention from her dad Hershel. At first Maggie seemed like a stuck up charcater because she was kind of cold to Glenn and the group But we later see how caring she is and how she just wanted her family to be protected. Maggie started off needing to be protected by Ricks group because she couldn't kill a mere walker to being known as The Widow. A woman who used her pain to fuel her strength and wanted to get revenge on Negan. She also became Hilltops leader.
