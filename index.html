Why You Should Visit Antigua and Barbuda

Two Islands One Flag

Antigua and Barbuda are two seperate islands in the carribean that make up one nation. The countries are reffered to as Antigua AND Barbuda because they are sister islands. This basically means the have a shared flag, national anthems, national dishes. This website will tell you about the places you can visit on both islands

Places to go!

Pink sand beach in Barbuda. Pink sand beach stretches for 8 miles between Spanish Point and Palmetto Point and can be visited as part of a boat trip to the nearby Frigate Bird sanctuary.

Tradewinns hotel is a beautiful resort in Antigua. Enjoy a beautiful and lively hotel with amazing breakfast a pool.

Carnival is a Carribean celebration that starts in July and ends the First tuesday in August. Many people travel to Antigua to experience Antigua's Carnival.

Things to eat!

Guinep is a fruit found in the carribeean. It has really sweet taste and refreshing taste to it so it easy to get addicted. This fruit is also very good for your health. Guinep helps lower blood pressure.

Goat water is the national cuisine of the Caribbean island of Montserrat.You can find goat water in antigua. Goat water is a spicy stew like dish that has goat meat,herbs and sometimes dumplings. Goat water is commonly enjoyed with bread or rice.
